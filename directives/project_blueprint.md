# Concentrates Marketplace – B2B Interface Blueprint

## Overview
This marketplace mirrors the familiarity and procurement flow of LeafLink but is purpose-built for bulk cannabis concentrates. The platform prioritizes price transparency, volume visibility, and efficient wholesale transactions between licensed extractors and licensed buyers.

## Top Navigation Bar
Logo | Browse Products | Suppliers | Orders | Messages | Reports | Notifications | Account (Company Dropdown)

## Left Sidebar (Category Navigation)
**Product Types:** Live Resin, Live Rosin, Distillate, Isolate, Sauce, Diamonds.
**Filters:** THC % Range, CBD %, Price per lb/kg, Volume Available, State, COA Verified Only, Organic / Solventless, New Listings, Price Drops.

## Main Marketplace View
The core marketplace allows users to toggle between Grid View and Table View depending on preference.

### Option A: Grid View (Brand-Forward Cards)
Each listing card includes Supplier Name, Product Type, THC %, Price per lb/kg, Available Volume, Location, COA Verified Badge.
**Action Buttons:** View COA, Request Sample, Add to Cart, Submit Offer.

### Option B: Table View (Procurement-Friendly)
Columns include Supplier, Product, THC %, Volume, Price, COA Status, Action.
**Example:** 
- Platinum Extracts | Live Resin | 85% | 30 lbs | $450/lb | Verified | Add to Cart.
- GreenLeaf Labs | Distillate | 92% | 50 kg | $320/kg | Verified | Submit Offer.

## Product Detail Page
**Supplier Header:** Logo, License Number, State, Rating, Average Response Time.
**Product Overview:** Product Type, Extraction Method, Cannabinoid Profile, Terpene Profile, Batch ID, Harvest Date, Available Volume.
**Pricing Section (Tiered Example):** 1–10 lbs → $450/lb; 10–25 lbs → $420/lb; 25+ lbs → $390/lb. Minimum Order Quantity clearly displayed.
**Documents Section:** View COA, Download Lab Report, View Safety Documentation.
**Primary Actions:** Request Sample, Add to Cart, Submit Offer, Message Supplier.

## Cart & Checkout Flow
Multi-supplier cart enabled. Minimum Order Quantities enforced. Delivery date selector. Net terms (if approved buyer). ACH payment option. Optional escrow trust badge for higher-value transactions.

## Dashboards
### Buyer Dashboard
Open Orders. Saved Suppliers. Reorder Quick List. Recently Viewed Products. Price Alerts. Market Price Trends.

### Seller Dashboard
Inventory Overview. Active Listings. Offers Received. Message Center.
**Analytics:** Listing Views, Offer Rate, Average Market Price vs Listed Price, Conversion Rate.

## Positioning vs LeafLink
LeafLink focuses primarily on finished goods and brand catalogs. This marketplace focuses exclusively on bulk concentrates and price discovery.
**Differentiation:** Real-time price comparison, Market average per concentrate type, Excess inventory spotlight section, Reverse auction functionality, Spot-market style bidding.

## Design Personality
Industrial. Data-driven. Efficient. Transactional. Less lifestyle branding, more pricing clarity and procurement efficiency.

## Layout Hierarchy
- **Top:** Filters and Search.
- **Left:** Categories and Filter Panel.
- **Center:** Listings (Grid or Table).
- **Right (Optional):** Market Insights Panel.
